Oronno Sohel Aungkon

Bangladesh's Pioneer Young Bassist

Childhood and Family

Oronno was born in **Bandarban**, a part of Bangladesh renowned for its natural beauty and cultural diversity, on **September 4, 2010**. Both her mother, **Salina Akter**, and father, **Gazi Mohammad Sohel**, are well-known for their commitment and share the same line of work. Samira Sohel Othoi, his older sister, has been a pillar of support and care throughout his life. Oronno, the youngest child in the family, grew up in a setting that valued learning, creativity, and curiosity. He developed a love for music at a young age and became the **first child bassist in Bangladesh**, gaining notoriety for his exceptional skill and daring foray into the music industry.

A Musical Adventure

When Oronno picked up the guitar for the first time in **2017**, his musical career officially began. He actively influenced the early sound and identity of the band **Khola Chhad Unlimited** by taking on the roles of lead guitarist and vocalist by **2019**. He developed a strong interest in **bass guitar** in **2020**, influenced by both famous Bangladeshi bands like **Warfaze, Aurthohin, and Artcell**, as well as international heavyweights like **Flea and John Myung**. Through practice, observation, and ear training, he taught himself how to play the bass.

Oronno became the youngest bassist to perform on a national television platform in Bangladesh in **2023** when he appeared on **Duronto TV** with Khola Chhad Unlimited. He formally became the band's bassist by **2025**, opening a new chapter in their shared musical history.

With the release of **four solo tracks and one song in 2025**, Oronno showcased his distinct identity as an instrumental storyteller and musician who skillfully combines emotion, intensity, and melody in each note.

Beliefs and Personality

Oronno is renowned for leading a tidy and focused life. He maintains **strong morals**, abstaining from alcohol and tobacco, honoring discipline, and valuing lifelong learning. Whether it's a song, a design, or a conversation, he feels that everything in life should be done with **honesty, purpose, and dedication**.

Vision and Legacy

Oronno Sohel Aungkon is a symbol of Bangladesh's emerging youth; they are bold, gifted, and have a strong sense of belonging and creativity. He uses technology, music, and teamwork to **empower young minds** with the goal of making **Bandarban a center of opportunity**.

Oronno, who is still a teenager, aspires to be one of Bangladesh's best bassists, not only because of his talent but also because he wants to encourage others to pursue their own artistic endeavors. Driven by **passion, purpose, and unwavering dedication**, his journey is just getting started and is full of opportunities.
