Biography

Oronno Sohel Aungkon

Early Life & Family

Orono was born on 4 September 2010 in Bandarban a region celebrated for its natural beauty and cultural richness in Bangladesh. Her father, Gazi Mohammad Sohel and her mother, Salina Akter, both work in the same profession and are highly respected for their dedication. He has an elder sister, Samira Sohel Othoi, whose constant care and support has been a guiding force in his life. Growing up as the youngest in the family, Orono was raised in an environment that encouraged creativity, curiosity and learning. With an early passion for music, he made history by becoming Bangladesh's first child bassist, earning recognition for his unique talent and fearless entry into the country's music scene.

Musical Journey

Oronno's musical journey began in 2017 when he started playing the guitar for the first time. By 2019, he became the vocalist and lead guitarist of the band Khola Chhad Unlimited, and actively shaped the band's early sound and identity. In 2020, his keen interest in bass guitar took hold inspired by global giants like Flea and John Myung as well as iconic Bangladeshi bands like Warfaze, Aurthohin and Artcell. He taught himself bass by ear, observation and constant practice.

In 2023, Oronno made his first national television appearance with Khola Chhad Unlimited on Duronto TV, becoming the youngest bassist to perform on such a platform in Bangladesh. By 2025, he officially joined the band as their bassist, adding a new chapter to their collective musical journey.

In 2025, Orono released four solo tracks and 1 song, where he expressed his unique identity as a musician and instrumental storyteller who blends melody, intensity and emotion in every note.

Vision & Legacy

Oronno Sohel Aungkon represents the rising youth of Bangladesh — fearless, talented, and deeply rooted in community and creativity. With a vision to transform Bandarban into a hub of opportunity, he empowers young minds through music, technology, and collaborative innovation.

Though still in his teenage years, Oronno dreams of becoming one of the finest bassists in Bangladesh — not just through skill, but by inspiring others to follow their own creative paths. His journey is only beginning, and the road ahead is filled with endless possibilities, driven by passion, purpose, and unwavering dedication.
