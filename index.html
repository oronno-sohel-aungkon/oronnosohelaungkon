Overview

Oronno Sohel Aungkon (born September 04, 2009) is a Bangladeshi bassist, composer, lyricist, and creative entrepreneur based in Bandarban. Known as the first kid bassist of Bangladesh, he is reshaping the country’s music and digital scene with his bold creativity and youthful energy.
