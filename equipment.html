The Musician's Arsenal

Oronno's Gear Setup

A look at the instruments, amplifiers, and effects that shape the unique sound of Oronno Sohel Aungkon, both on stage and in the studio.

Primary Instrument

Fender Jazz Bass (Modded)

The backbone of the live sound. Heavily modified with custom pickups and a pre-amp for unmatched tonal versatility, from funk slaps to deep grooves.

USA Deluxe Series Specs

Powerhouse

Ampeg SVT-CL Classic

The legendary 300W all-tube amp, providing the classic, growling, and punchy tone needed for arena-sized sound and studio warmth.

300 Watt Tube Specs

Effects Chain

Darkglass Alpha Omega

A custom-built pedal board centered around the Darkglass Alpha Omega for modern distortion and drive, supplemented by a Wah and Octave pedal.

Distortion & FX Specs

Unplugged Sessions

Warwick StarBass

Used primarily for recording acoustic tracks and intimate unplugged performances. Provides a woody, deep, and resonant tone perfect for jazz fusion.

Semi-Hollow Body Specs

Home Studio

Focusrite Scarlett 18i20

High-fidelity audio interface crucial for recording demos and final tracks with minimal latency and crystal-clear preamps.

Ampeg 8x10 Cabinet

The classic 8x10 speaker cabinet, known as the "Fridge." Essential for moving serious air and translating the SVT-CL's power into earth-shaking bass.

The Philosophy of Tone

Oronno believes that gear is just a tool, but the right tools can unlock new sonic possibilities. Every piece of equipment in this collection is chosen not for its price tag, but for its unique contribution to the final, expressive tone.
